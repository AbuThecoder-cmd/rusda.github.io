Implementing Partners Past and Related Work Experience

An assessment of the existing producer associations and Opportunities for formation of collective
marketing associations to integrate refugees and stallholder farmers into sustainable collective
marketing in the rhino camp refugee settlement areas

Working and developing Local seed business in the region, RUSDA realised to form a cooperative. In
2016 working in promoting marketing of QDS RUSDA ltd in partnership with CHAIN Uganda
formed initiated formation of West Nile Seed Multiplication Cooperative Society Ltd that has
marketed 9,032 bags of Cassava cuttings from 127.6 out of 175.5 acres worth UGX 270,960,000 from
LSBs, Lead farmers. 1T of sesame II from 2 local seed business groups to various development
partners like GoU, Smaritan purse, DRC, Mercy corps etc

In 2017 RUSDA under the consortium implemented Soy bean Value development in 8 Districts of
West Nile region. A total of 30 agronomic trials and 47 demonstration sites with various inputs to
understand yield differences in soy bean are being shown to 100 farmers groups to make correct
agronomy decisions. This is being funded by NUTEC-Palladium program in Northern Uganda.
